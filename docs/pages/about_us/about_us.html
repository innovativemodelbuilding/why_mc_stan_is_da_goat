About Us

This website is made by @Arcturus_dhh to record all of MC Stan’s achievements in one place. Every chart-topping single, every record shattered, and every award won is tried to documented here so you have a clear view of why MC Stan commands such respect in the desi hip hop scene.

Our mission is to educate fans and newcomers alike on his music and influence. Explore deep dives into his albums, read the untold stories behind his lyrics, and discover how MC Stan’s raw energy and authenticity have reshaped the scene and inspired a new generation of artists.

You’ll also find curated links to his most iconic Instagram Lives, the memes that capture his larger-than-life persona, and a collection of moments that show why MC Stan’s legacy is more than just music. it’s a movement. Welcome to the definitive MC Stan archive.
